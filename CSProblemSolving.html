Activity 1

Person 1= 1min

Person 2= 2mins

Person 3= 5mins

Person 4= 10mins

Person 1 will act as a courier that will carry the lantern. Since he is the fastest person, he will be able to carry the lantern back to the next person in the shortest amount of time. Therefore the math would be 10+1+5+1+2=19. The +1 are the trips he takes to carry the lantern back to the next person.

Activity 2

  • Get 2 ropes, at the same time light both ends of the first rope and one end of  the second rope on fire.

  • When the first rope is completely burnt, 30 minutes have passed and the second rope has 30 minutes worth of rope left to burn. Light the other end of the second rope on fire at this time.

  • Since the second rope had 30 minutes worth of rope left to burn and both ends are burning after the last step, the second rope will be completely burnt in another 15 minutes.

  • The 15 minutes from the last step and the 30 minutes for the previous makes for 45 minutes, the exact time to solve the puzzle.

  • At this time you would push the lever back into place, opening the door.
